Work

I run and advise businesses in trading, finance, and marketing:

  • Rank — Co-Founder. A decentralized algorithmic trading platform for professional traders and investors. Rank lets traders build, backtest, and deploy strategies without code, while investors get transparent performance, risk metrics, and real-time reporting.
  • Getsé Trading — Co-Founder. A crypto hedge fund specializing in systematic, quantitative strategies across the top digital assets, currently managing over $20M AUM.
  • 3Statki — Owner. A full-service marketing agency focused on the Polish market. I no longer run operations day-to-day, but still steer strategy. Before 3Statki, I ran a successful international agency working with clients across multiple industries.

At Rank and Getse I lead product, infrastructure, growth and finance. I have written code, architected systems, raised capital and built teams. My focus now is growing Getse’s AUM and managing Rank’s product. Rank has a leading vault product that allows investors to access yield from professional strategies. Getse manages the vault to generate returns in a fully transparent and systematic way.
